Calling all startup-minded individuals and those who have a Product/POC/MVP to pitch. 

The startups get to present their product/service to Investors/Mentors and peers. Everybody works as a team to refine the idea and also discuss opportunities for growth. 

And the attendees not presenting get a chance to know whats happening in the startup eco-system and network with relevant mentors.

At Startup Meetup we host startups at prototypes/strategies stage from varying domains like logistics, robotics, online communities, business social platforms, Socially Driven Adventure, Adventure Travel, EdTech, Handicrafts Ecommerce and HRTech among other promising startups. Startups have been able to get seed funding, find co-founders, get improvement in their pitches, network with like minded startups and investors and become an alliance to enhance their market presence. Knowledge sharing by mentors has seen topics covered like legal for startups, GST deep dive, product - market fit and when to start looking for Investment. 

Join us in this constructive critique session that promises investor connect to the star products.

Presentation Format: 

You get 10 minutes for a Powerful Introduction and 5 minutes Q&A to field queries and critiques. This is followed by networking over coffee and grub, where you get a chance to meet other startups, investors and like-minded individuals.

Who Can Participate:

• All those with a pitch, prototype, MVP ready for pitching on this platform. 

• All who want to know whats happening in the startup ecosystem.
